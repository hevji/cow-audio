# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This is a small open source project. We want it to be a welcoming place for anyone who wants to contribute, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

Be respectful. Be constructive. Assume good intent.

## Expected behavior

- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Focus on the work, not the person
- Be patient with newcomers
- Accept that people have different approaches and that's fine

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Dismissing contributors because of their experience level
- Sustained disruptive behavior in issues or pull requests

## Enforcement

If someone is behaving in a way that makes the project unwelcoming, their comments or contributions may be removed and they may be blocked from the repository. This will always be a last resort.

## Reporting

If something happens that you think violates this code of conduct, open a private security advisory or contact the maintainer directly through GitHub.
